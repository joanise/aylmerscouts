Fundraising events fall 2024 – 1st Aylmer Scouts

Fundraising helps make Scouting more affordable, supporting camps, materials and special events for all. This fall, 3 fundraisers are active. Please consider whether one of these might be of interest to you, and you are invited to spread the word to the wider community.

Oranges and grapefruit

This is our 3rd year with the citrus fundraiser. This year we are aiming to have orders delivered in time for Christmas.

The quality of the fruit has been excellent, as it arrives fresh from the orchard. If you have room to refrigerate, fruit will keep at least a month. Our supplier is The Orange Barn, and they import directly from California (oranges) and Texas (grapefruit). Typically, there are 72 oranges in a 40 lb case. Grapefruit numbers are more variable, but you can expect around 36-40 in a 40 lb case. Case prices are competitive with grocery store prices – you may want to consider splitting one with a friend.

Order deadline is November 10. Expect to pick up your order in Aylmer the week of December 9. An exact date will be shared once our delivery is scheduled.

Payment can be made by e-transfer to oranges@aylmerscouts.ca. If you would like to support a particular section, give us the name of your salesperson or let us know which section (Beavers, Cubs, Scouts or Venturers).

Christmas trees

Bôsapin will deliver a Christmas tree to your door. If you are home, the Nutcracker will even bring it inside for you. Ordering couldn’t be simpler, just go to our dedicated portal on their website and browse the options. Popular choices do run out, so order early for the best selection.

Knights of Columbus state lottery

The Knights of Columbus (Chevaliers de Colomb) annual raffle gives a chance at 15 different cash and other prizes. Tickets are $2 each, 3 for $5, and $10 for a booklet of 6 (ages 18 and up only). All profits go to charity: 60% of our ticket sales will go to 1st Aylmer. 1st Aylmer youth will have tickets for sale until mid December. The prize draw will be April 4, 2025.
